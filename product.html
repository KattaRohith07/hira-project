ABOUT US

Hira - The Security Assistant is a state-of-the-art dash cam designed to revolutionize road safety. Equipped with advanced technology, Hira goes beyond traditional dash cams, serving as a vigilant guardian on the road. Its sophisticated sensors detect signs of driver drowsiness, alerting motorists in real-time to prevent potential accidents caused by fatigue. Moreover, Hira's intelligent system swiftly identifies and predicts impending collisions, enabling proactive intervention to avert accidents before they occur. By seamlessly integrating cutting-edge innovation with user-friendly design, Hira not only safeguards lives but also transforms driving experiences, instilling confidence and peace of mind for every journey. With Hira by your side, the road becomes a safer place for all, where accidents are minimized, and lives are protected.
